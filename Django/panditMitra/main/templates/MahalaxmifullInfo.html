Description

Mahalaxmi Puja is performed for Goddess Laxmi, the consort of Lord Vishnu is a manifestation of wealth, fortune, and prosperity. People who are involved in business or people who are facing financial problems may perform this puja to receive the blessings and grace of Goddess Laxmi and attain high revenues & profitability in business. This puja is performed by invoking Goddess Laxmi followed by chanting Laxmi Mantra and then performing the Puja to receive Goddess Laxmi’s blessings. When to Perform Kubera Laxmi havan? Schedule the havan date as per the Janma Nakshatra of a person and by considering the Yoga, and Thithi of that particular date. Diwali, Dhanteras, Akshaya Tritiya, Fridays are considered auspicious times to perform havan.
